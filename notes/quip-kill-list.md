# QUIP KILL-LIST — Parliament of the Snake

## Summary

**49 instances total: 28 KILL / 9 MOVE / 11 KEEP / 1 AUTHOR'S CALL**

Sweeper census (45 entries) merged with the 8 reader-panel confirmations. Overlaps deduped: panel's 183, 189, and 422 (= census 421, one-off numbering drift) were already in the census — sweepers and panel agree on all three. No sweeper/panel disagreements found.

### Three highest-impact kills

1. **Snippet 78** — the fireworks gag after the Clearings/deportations rage monologue. The exact confirmed-pattern case, and it flattens the book's central political horror at its very first full statement.
2. **Snippet 557** — "We're in the next bit" fired in the same breath as Baker collapsing under torture that weaponises a dead friend's screams. The darkest beat in the manuscript, deflated by a fourth-wall wink.
3. **Snippet 596** — Wozzy's "SIXTEEN POUNDS. I HAVE A KNIFE." landing the instant the villain dies. The textbook pun-straight-after-a-death-scene this book is known for, on the climactic kill.
