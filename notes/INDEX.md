# Notes Index — *Parliament of the Snake*

Ordered by when you'd reach for it: daily snippet editing → the rebuild → the prequel.

## Daily snippet editing (diagnosis)

- **reader-report.md** — four reader personas' verdicts on the full manuscript by quarter; where each thought it sang and where it sagged.
- **scene-map.md** — the corkboard: every scene as a one-line beat with cast + location, snippet-numbered.
- **repetition-report.md** — merge targets for redundant scenes (John's morning routine x4, etc.), ranked by how much cutting they need, with what's worth saving from each.
- **character-threads.md** — on-page/gap ranges per major character, so you can see who's absent from a stretch before you cut it.
- **tenet-audit.md** — hub page: verdicts-at-a-glance for the 5 (of 6) core tenets, linking out to `tenets/`.
- **tenet-audit's tenets/ subfiles** — full evidence + line-level fixes per tenet:
  - **incompetent-dystopia.md** — regime's rubbish-dystopia bit; best-executed tenet, just needs the incompetence doctrine stated earlier.
  - **it-brain-tank.md** — ThinkTank-as-brain-in-a-tank rewrite: what's already seeded vs. what needs planting for the reveal.
  - **john-hiding.md** — John's "hiding not scared" read vs. the competing grief explanation that currently crowds it out.
  - **main-characterness.md** — every instance of John's inexplicable narrative-protagonist luck, rated invisible/comic/plot-convenient.
  - **meta-layer.md** — Vetko's book-awareness lines, rated deniable vs. too-loud, snippet by snippet.
  - **music-friendship.md** — music-as-friendship-glue audit; wired everywhere but silent at the four biggest John/Reza emotional beats.
  - **raven-erica.md** — the Raven-was-Erica thread map: all eight deniable touches plus the two things the prequel must square.
- **quip-kill-list.md** — 49 flagged jokes (28 kill / 9 move / 11 keep / 1 author's call), with the three highest-impact kills up top.
- **continuity-report.md** — hard contradictions across slices (Cesira's days in power, Reza's age) that need one canonical answer.

## The Rebuild (the main working documents)

- **rebuild/briefs-001-064.md** through **rebuild/briefs-577-624.md** (9 files, ~64 snippets each) — per-scene work orders: verdict (keep/trim/cut), what happens, panel feedback, required fixes, and dependencies on neighboring scenes. This is where the diagnosis above gets turned into actual rebuild instructions, scene by scene, for the Scrivener pass.

## The Prequel (once book two is settled)

- **prequel-bible.md** — Beck Avon & Doug Goode premise and hard constraints: everything book two already commits to on the page that the prequel can't contradict (Cesira's rule, the Realists' pitch, the Raven's disappearance timeline, etc.).
