# Tenet Audit — Parliament of the Snake

Five of six core tenets audited against the manuscript, snippet numbers matching the editing tool. Full evidence and suggested edits in `notes/tenets/`. The sixth (IT = the Pulpos brain in a tank, cipher inventory) is pending — the audit hit a usage limit mid-report and will be re-run.

## Verdicts at a glance

**1. The dystopia is shit at its job** → [tenets/incompetent-dystopia.md](tenets/incompetent-dystopia.md)
Already the most consistently executed idea in the book — budget/austerity rot runs through drones, checkpoints and ministries like a motif, and snippets 303 ("they've seen all the missing screws") and 508 state the doctrine outright. Problem is placement: both explicit statements arrive in the back half, so Act One incompetence can read as sloppiness. And Vetko's departure is never tied to it — snippet 77 gives no reason for the blimp. Five sentence-level edits fix it; the highest-value is one rumour-line at 77.

**2. The meta layer (Vetko knows)** → [tenets/meta-layer.md](tenets/meta-layer.md)
The dialogue layer is dosed almost perfectly — "Because its what I'm supposed to do" (555), "How else could this end?" (609), "You were never that important" (610) are exactly right. Two problems: (i) two explicit structural tells twelve snippets apart ("We're in the next bit" 557, "this scenes finished" 611) — one too many; (ii) Vetko's first on-page impossibility is the knife catch at 610 with zero precedent, which is why readers called it unmotivated. Fix is pattern, not explanation: plant folklore early (15), "It always did" at 450, rain not landing on him at 606. Also: "You're only fucking here" (612) — the most important line in the layer — currently parses as a typo.

**3. The Raven was Erica** → [tenets/raven-erica.md](tenets/raven-erica.md)
The threads already touch eight times, always deniably — the bucket fall at 298 ("The Ravens dead"), "she died a long time ago" at 585, the hair-dye chain (dark-haired Raven 73 → bottle-blonde Erica 146 → "wasn't sure about the blond" 528), Vetko and John both weighing photos of the same dead woman (546/527), the interrupted psychics. Nothing on the page contradicts the tenet. Two flags: the timeline pinches (Erica has ~1-2 years between the Raven vanishing and dying — the illness arc reads multi-year), and the Raven's public face (posters, pogs) vs Erica's client-facing job is the one thing the prequel must square. Six deniable seeds suggested.

**4. John is hiding, not scared** → [tenets/john-hiding.md](tenets/john-hiding.md)
The evidence is all on the page — showing his pass to a dead camera (53), the witness-counting litanies, "echoes of old duties" (435), "after all this time they were going to find him" (496), Reza's "packed a bag and done one. Again" (528), the psychics' "man on the run… a man who is another man" (335-336) — but it's back-loaded, and the narrator's grief-cocoon diagnosis at 183 crowds it out early. Through snippet 250 the coward read is the correct read of the page. Six early tilts fix it; the cheapest and biggest is extending snippet 95: "Eight years of nothing happening. People assume nothing happens by itself. Nothing takes work."

**5. Music carries the friendships** → [tenets/music-friendship.md](tenets/music-friendship.md)
Music soundtracks every John/Reza logistical scene but is silent in all four of their emotional peaks (bunk-bed, violinist reveal, hand-holding, pub chase). No song recurs between them, and the concert-violinist reveal (530) currently explains only Reza's accent. Meanwhile music-vs-Harmony opposition is implicit five times and never cashed. Five fixes, all one-to-three lines: a recurring song (The Passenger, 224 → 496), Reza humming something bowed in the bunk-bed dark, a hummed callback at the hand-holding, "You can pick the music" as the book's last friendship line (623), and — the big one — Reza's illegal banger clicking on in the dead silence after the Harmony hum stops (595).

**6. IT = the Pulpos brain in a tank** → [tenets/it-brain-tank.md](tenets/it-brain-tank.md)
The manuscript is astonishingly ready for this rewrite — snippet 508 already canonizes it ("We got rid of the Pulpos." / "You caused the Pulpos." / "A necessary test."), the ThinkTank is never physically shown (a clean slot for the tank), and Baker's ceiling-crawl is already octopus body-horror. The cipher decodes as A1Z26 ("Behold It") and the audit found an escalation arc from BEHOLD ME to IT IS OMNIPOTENT IT IS HERE (posted 8 times the moment before Julie is removed from the group chat) — plus three encoding typos to fix (62, 106, 124) and one corrupted block to re-encode (98). Full verbatim number-string inventory included.

**7. Main-characterness (Erica → John; Vetko wants it; Palm knows)** → PENDING
Audit running: where the power visibly operates, Palm's uncanniness inventory, traces of the transfer at Erica's death, whether the rooftop reads as Vetko locating the mantle. Constraint applied: hints and weirdness only — no payoff in this book (prequel: the Raven/Beck Avon and Doug Goode; meta pays off in book three).

## The cross-tenet pattern

Every audit reached the same conclusion independently: **the material is already on the page; what's missing is early declaration.** The incompetence needs claiming in Act One, the meta needs one early folk-rumour, the hiding needs one early tell, the music needs one recurring theme. The book's back half consistently pays off things its front half never visibly bought. Almost all suggested edits are single sentences in existing scenes — phone-editor-sized.
