# Tenet Audit: "IT Is a Giant Brain in a Tank"

**Tenet (planned rewrite):** The ThinkTank is literally the brain of the Pulpos — the last running AI in charge of everything is a brain in a jar that CAUSED the Pulpos incident. It has infected Ian Baker's mind. It speaks through Harmony in the govchats. All the number strings are a cipher. The H'gok/Lovecraft material will be rewritten to reflect this.

## 4. THE REWRITE MAP

### (a) Must change for H'gok → brain-in-tank

- **603–605** — the Lovecraft swerve (Department of Eldritch Horrors, H'gok, eyeball-wheels): rewrite so what's behind Brad's door is Pulpos-programme material; "two weeks was enough" points at the Two Weeks of Tentacles, not a god.
- **2** — "bejewelled dagger of **H'gok**" — retune or cut the name (the Apothis gag survives).
- **277** — "the unspeakable death god of g'kakak" — repoint at the thing behind the vault door.
- **272** — replace embedded author notes with actual scene; dramatize the **IT Department** pun here.
- **587–596** — the Control Room: the tank must physically appear. Imagery is halfway there ("tendrils growing from a bolted potato… An infesting fungus") — make the tendrils run down into glass.
- **595** — "Terminate Connection Permanently?" — keep, but it should read as unplugging a *mind*.
- **589–592** — Baker-on-ceiling: salvageable. With the brain established, it becomes Pulpo-possession — boneless wall-crawling, colour-shifting eyes are already octopus body-horror (plus 556's "muscles roiling"). Adjust flavour, not structure.
- **599, 612–614** — decide the brain's fate; the 272 note says "The think tank, its escaping," which has no on-page counterpart at all.
- **546** — standardize It capitalization; "I don't care whats in there" lands harder once "in there" means a tank.
- Scratch **637** and **632** ("Thinktank is the only thing not peace ministry" — contradicts 468) need reconciling.

### (c) Seeding suggestions — inevitable-in-hindsight

1. **The water bill (77 or 277).** The "super computer" is the only department in Whitehall with a *water* budget — filed under "aquarium maintenance," renewed annually, queried by no one. Pays off at the vault door, which could hum "like a filter pump."
2. **The procurement meeting (277).** The Department of Meetings You Probably Don't Need already buys "flour and ketamine." Add brine, algae blocks, and "40kg frozen mackerel (recurring, do not query)." The pickled brain running the country has a standing supplier arrangement and a preferred-vendor form.
3. **Julie gets one line closer to the truth (15 or 25).** "You don't *eradicate* something that clever, Paul Bond. You give it a government job." The reveal retroactively crowns the govchat's paranoid aunt as the only person who was right.
4. **Baker feeds the jar (267).** Baker absent-mindedly taps the half-Pulpo's glass mid-sentence; Roger notices Dymo tape: "SAMPLE 44. DECEASED. DO NOT WATER." Welds Frequency 44 to Pulpo tissue two hundred pages early.
5. **One spam menu item turns literal (42/51/62).** "*Octopus Pakora £0.00 — It remembers the sea. It remembers everything.*" Naj: "Ah, ah, who even sells octopus anymore?" — bot removed from group. The reader who cracks the cipher gets rewarded twice.

**Structural note:** the joke's engine — Britain's last functioning AI is a brain in a jar that caused the disaster it was promoted for solving — is already fully load-bearing in 508's three-line exchange. The rewrite mostly needs the tank's physical appearance (587-region) to make every govchat curry ad, every thrum, and Julie's "its in my head" click into place at once.
